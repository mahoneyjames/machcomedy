ANGELA BARNES

ANGELA BARNES

Sunday 5th May 7.00 PM

Machynlleth Rugby Club

£5.00

  • Bio
  • Links
  • Goodies

Join 2011 BBC New Comedy Award winner Angela Barnes for her debut solo hour in which she talks about the big stuff, you know, life, death, love, work and how rubbish she probably is at all of it...

“Brilliantly funny” Sarah Millican

“Hers is a world-weary and devastatingly matter-of-fact outlook on the world that really makes her stand out” The Guardian

This show will be an hour long.

This show is for over 16's only.

Additional booking fees are not being charged by the festival this year, but are being incorporated into the overall advertised ticket price. Please see the website for further information.
